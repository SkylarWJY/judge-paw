⚖️ 🐾  The First AI Relationship Court

Judge Paw

When couples fight, there's no neutral third party.
So you sulk, you guess, or you ask the group chat —
and next week, you have the exact same fight.

The Solution

Both sides are heard.
One fair verdict.

Each partner states their case. Judge Paw weighs them both — and rules.

⚖️ Ruling 🧠 Reasoning 💕 Sentence

Fair, warm, and never unfairly takes a side.

A Real Case
Partner A
"I was just relaxing after a long week."
⚖️
Partner B
"I did everything around the house — alone."
Judge Paw names a valid point from each of them — so nobody walks away unheard — then hands down a kind sentence. Case closed. 🐾
